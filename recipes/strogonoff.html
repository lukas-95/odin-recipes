How to make

First

In a large skillet brown your meat in oil over medium-high heat. When meat is browned (a little croast), drain excess fat from skillet. Reserve the meat in a bowl. Pay attention, too much meat on your skillet won't deliver an amazing croast. When you finish your last round of meat, add onion (in cubes), garlic (I prefer the slightest slices), salt and pepper and stir in.

Second

Add mushrooms, bell pepper (yellow, red, green), 3 sp of mayo, 1 sp of ketchup/barbecue 1/2 sp of mustard. Remove from heat, add sour cream, the amount depends on how creamy you want. Stir everything, serve with chips and withe rice

Ingredients

  • 600gr Meat
  • 4 cloves of garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 onion
  • 100gr fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 200ml sour cream
  • Potato chips
  • White rice

Directions

  1. In a large skillet brown your meat in oil over medium-high heat. When meat is browned (a little croast), drain excess fat from skillet.
  2. Reserve the meat in a bowl. Pay attention, too much meat on your skillet won't deliver an amazing croast.
  3. When you finish your last round of meat, add onion (in cubes), garlic (I prefer the slightest slices), salt and pepper and stir in.
  4. Add mushrooms, bell pepper (yellow, red, green), 3 sp of mayo, 1 sp of ketchup/barbecue 1/2 sp of mustard.
  5. Remove from heat, add sour cream, the amount depends on how creamy you want. Stir everything, serve with chips and withe rice
